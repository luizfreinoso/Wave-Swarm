--------------- Wave Swarm --------------------

This game was made in the UCL university from Manguinhos, ES - Brazil during the Global Jam 2017 event.


The theme of the Jam was Waves, and the team resposible for developing this game though in the idea of doing a game where the player have to reach the Goddess Iemanjá while jumping over a few waves in order to offer her an present in order to stop the waves.

The Player must press space in order to jump.
